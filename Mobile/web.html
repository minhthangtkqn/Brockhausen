Brockhausen Engineering supplies engineering, technical know-how, complete turnkey projects, machines and wear resistant cast parts for the cement and mineral processing industries, especially for grinding plants.
The company takes the chalenge to supply grinding systems and equipment that guarantees lowest production cost for our customers. Based on decades of experience and in co-operation with customers, universities and research institutions we continuously work on research and development projects.

Main aspects of these projecrs are: Development of new and improved machines like seperator, cooler and drier integration of research partners with trial plants for the development of new mill internals, especially mill shell lining systems and mill diaphragms use of newest results of material science for the development of new wear resistant cast materials with improved combinations of characteristic.

ball mill internals

Welche
Headline?

Brockhausen Engineering supplies complete internals for ball mills. The package of mill internals consists mainly of: mill shell lining (PAL or DFL system), inlet-, discharge-, outlet- and other diaphragms, grinding media, mill inlet systems and water injection systems.

Our own developments of mill internals aim to reduce the specific energy consumption.

As energy consumption and wear are in direct relation, out energy-saving grinding systems also reduce wear and therewith the operating costs.
